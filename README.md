# 📊 Vivek E-commerce Sales Dashboard  

This is my **first Power BI project** where I created an interactive dashboard to analyze online sales across India.  

---

## 📌 Objective  
The owner of **Madhav Store** wanted a dashboard to track and analyze their **online sales performance** across:  
- Different states  
- Product categories & subcategories  
- Payment modes  
- Monthly sales & profit trends  

---

## 🛠️ Project Learnings  
- Created an **interactive dashboard** to track and analyze sales data  
- Used **filters and slicers** for drill-down and customization  
- Built **relationships, joins, and calculated fields** to clean and manipulate data  
- Implemented **parameters** to allow user-driven analysis  
- Designed using **multiple chart types**:  
  Bar chart | Pie chart | Donut chart | Clustered bar chart | Line chart | Area chart | Map | Slicers  

---


## 🚀 Tools Used  
- **Power BI** (for data visualization)  
- **Excel/CSV** (data source)  

---

## 📢 Key Insights  
- Maharashtra & Delhi were top-performing states in sales  
- Phones & Laptops contributed the highest profit  
- UPI & Cash were among the most preferred payment modes  
- Office Supplies had the maximum sales quantity
